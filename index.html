My Favourite Meal

Efo Riro

This rich vegetable soup is native to the Yoruba people of South-Western Nigeria. The word, is derived from two Yoruba words, efo which means green leafy vegetable and riro which means to stir. One can, therefore, deduce from this synergy that efo riro is leafy vegetable that is stirred in rich pepper stock.

Ingredients

The ingredients used to prepare ẹ̀fọ́ riro vary, especially in which meats are included, but some common ingredients are:

  • Beef
  • Cow tripe (shaki)
  • Cow skin (ponmo)
  • Smoked fish
  • Dry fish
  • Palm oil
  • Peppers (red bell peppers or chili peppers)
  • Ground crayfish
  • Onions
  • Bouillon cubes
  • Locust beans
  • Salt
  • Efo
